About Me

Hi, I'm Jen Strayer

I am Front-End Web Developer that is based just outside of Nashville, TN. I hold a degree in Music Education and Euphonium Performance from Ithaca College in Ithaca, NY, and, after teaching for five years throughout Middle Tennessee, I made the transition into website development. Outside of playing the euphonium, I also enjoy playing the ukulele, listening to Broadway musicals, and playing and watching ice hockey - often cheering on my favorite team, the Nashville Predators!
